RO.ME "3 DREAMS OF BLACK"

RO.ME is a really fun experience. It feels like it was way ahead of it's time and gives us a lot of insight into technology can be further improved upon. The visuals are stunning, and it feels like it inspired modern day VR experiences. The part with the city and animals walking around stood out the most, with the unique art style.

Technology: They primarily used WebGL to create the experience, along with HTML, JavaScript. They also take in feedback from the mouse, which I found interesting.
